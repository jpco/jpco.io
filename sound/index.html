Jack Conger: Theatre Artist

Current Projects

Right now I am working on the fight direction for Neighborhood 3: Requisition of Doom directed by Michael Hanley and produced by the University of Washington's UTS (Undergraduate Theatre Society) (link in the left sidebar).

I am also designing sound for UTS's production of The Picture of Dorian Grey, adapted by Neil Bartlett and directed by Rachel Perlot.

In a few months I will work as music director for the UTS production of Cabaret, directed by Elizabeth Schiffler, and after that, I will design sound for On the Verge by Eric Overmyer, directed by Rosalind Phelps.
